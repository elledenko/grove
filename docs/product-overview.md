# Grove — Product Overview

## Problem

People want to build self-care habits but clinical wellness apps feel cold and gamified ones feel childish. There's no web-first option that strikes the right tone for adults.

## Target User

Adults 22-40 — remote workers, knowledge workers, anyone with mild burnout or anxiety who wants gentle motivation without feeling patronized.

## Core Value Proposition

A web-first self-care companion app where you nurture a virtual plant that grows as you take care of yourself. Adult-friendly, calm aesthetic. Fast, minimal UX. Your plant is a living reflection of your self-care consistency.

## V0.1 Feature Set

**IN:**
- Email auth (sign up / login)
- Virtual plant companion with 5 growth stages (seed → sprout → sapling → bloom → tree)
- Daily self-care goal checklist (preset + custom goals)
- XP system — completing goals earns XP, XP grows your plant
- Mood check-in (select mood + optional note)
- Dashboard: plant status, streak counter, mood history chart, today's goals
- Streak tracking (consecutive days with at least 1 goal completed)
- Clean, minimal design — earth tones, soft greens, warm neutrals

**OUT (future cycles):**
- Social features / friend system
- Breathing exercises / guided meditation
- AI-powered recommendations
- Mobile app
- Premium tier / payments
- Journaling
- Multiple companions
- Achievements / badges

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: Next.js (App Router) + Tailwind CSS
- Backend: Supabase (Auth, Postgres)
- Deployment: Vercel
- Language: TypeScript

## Success Metric

A user can sign up, set goals, complete them, watch their plant grow, and track their mood — all in under 2 minutes per day.
